SUPERIOR DOUGH

We make the dough for our Roman-style pizza base in-house every day using the finest grade Italian flour and extra-virgin olive oil. Matured in 3 stages over 72 hours, it is hand tossed, stretched, topped and then stone baked in our custom-built Castelli oven from Rome.
